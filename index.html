Wine Quality and Climate

Wine is able to express a flavor profile that is representative of the environmental conditions the grape was grown in. This project intends to use monthly climatic variables in conjuction with certain wine characteristics to predict a numerical wine quality value.

Time-Series Analysis of the Texas Energy Grid

As extreme weather events become more common and the US energy grid infrastructure continues to age, it is imperative that the public makes informed decisions abou their power usage. This group project utilized hourly energy data to perform a time-series analysis and forecast future energy demand for the Texas energy grid.
